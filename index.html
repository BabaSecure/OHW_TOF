About The Foundation:

TOF is a Non-Governmental and Non-Religious Organization that is committed to developing and improving the management and leadership skills of existing and future leaders. We support individuals in shaping their leadership and career path and supporting the less-privileged by providing trainings that helps to secure a brighter future. We are aimed at raising young innovative leaders and building hopes. 

Our Goals:

  • To create a platform where individuals meet, learn and grow their leadership and professional career.
  •  To promote the Sustainable Developmental Goals 3, 4 and 6

Vision and Mission

  • To be a motivation to individual in awaken their leadership and career path
  • Encourage personal development of individuals
